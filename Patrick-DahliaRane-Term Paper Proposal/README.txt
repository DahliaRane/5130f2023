Web-Based Image Annotation Tool: Streamlining Visual Data Labeling with Modern Technology



I aspire for this proposed term paper to make significant contributions to the field of Web systems. The primary goal is to enhance the efficiency of data annotation, increase accessibility, and cater to the ever-expanding requirements of various industries. By addressing these pressing needs, I envision the proposed tool becoming an invaluable asset, offering a versatile, user-centric platform that streamlines the process of visual data labeling in our increasingly digital world.
